  Who are we?


   Origins


   Gro-Pro was founded in 2009 in Idaho by an enthusiastic entrepreneur called John Doe, who was frankly getting sick of having his plants die and not knowing the cause.


   Having been inspired off of plant meters, John made his own, including other programs to better suit people's needs.

   The product was a big success amongst young gardeners who were starting off on their gardening journey!
